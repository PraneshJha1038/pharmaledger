About PharmaLedger

PharmaLedger is a revolutionary healthcare technology platform designed to combat the growing threat of counterfeit medicines in India. Our mission is to create a safer healthcare ecosystem by providing transparent, secure, and accessible medicine verification tools.

Our Mission

To eliminate counterfeit medicines from the Indian market by creating a unified verification platform that connects all stakeholders in the pharmaceutical supply chain.

Our Vision

A future where every citizen can confidently verify the authenticity of their medicines, ensuring safe and effective healthcare for all.

The Problem

  • 1 in 10 medicines sold in India are counterfeit
  • $4.3 billion counterfeit drug market
  • 20-25% of global counterfeit drugs from India

Our Solution

  • Centralized verification database
  • QR code-based authentication
  • Multi-stakeholder platform
